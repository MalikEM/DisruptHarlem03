Shaun Pegg in Shaun of The Dead

+

Shaun Pegg, played by Simon Pegg, is a survivor of the zombie apocalypse. A slacker who cares for his friend and tasked with protecting them when the dead rise.

+

At the end of the movie, him and his girlfriend Liz return to a different world that now use the dead as a workforce.
