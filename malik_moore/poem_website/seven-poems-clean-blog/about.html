Seven Poems

Seven Poems is a website for anyone who wants to share a poem they have written while also being able to read some created by others. Also if you wish to find inspiration, want to relax, or find something to read briefly. The home page will always feature the seven poems posted everyday. On the Submit page, you can find out how to send a poem. You will also find the sites email to ask any further questions. On the Events page, you will find upcoming writing events where you can meet others who share your interest. We try to have as many upcoming events as possible to build up different writing communities.

Favorite Poems of (Month)

On the first day of each month, we have a page dedicated to poems from the previous month we personally liked and the original date they were posted. Please note the order of the poems on this site is not listed in a most/least favorite order but instead reflect the order in which they were sent.
