NYC Laid Back Writing Group

New York, NY

7 PM to 11 PM - 12/4/2016

This group has been around since 2002, with the purpose of having a relaxed setting where anyone can have a chance to hang out and meet fellow writers. This group is free, but because of the cost of advertising on Meetup, there's a suggested donation of $1 -- not per meeting, but just at the first one you attend.

Astoria Writers Group

Astoria, NY

5 PM to 8 PM - 12/4/2016

Astoria Writers Group are passionate writers looking to meet other passionate writers. Their group loves to have fun, with a atmosphere that is warm, welcoming, playful - at times even silly. At the same time, they offer sincere feedback and try to improve each other's work. The environment is truthful, but safe. Their hope is to create a nurturing community of writers who believe that writing is perhaps the most important thing in the world and who inspire one another to publish and to grow in their craft.

Harlem Neighborhood Writers

New York, NY

1:30 PM to 3 PM - 12/3/2016

Are you really where you want to be with your writing? Are you looking for a supportive group that wants to understand where you want to go with the writing you have? Just looking for some firm peer mentors to help you jump start the writing you want to do? Looking to escape the frenetic midtown writer hangouts? Then this meetup is for you! We meet in Harlem to sit and write quietly, followed by some discussion on what we're working on and a chance to socialize!

New York City Writers Critique Group

New York, NY

1:45 PM to 4:45 PM - 12/3/2016

We are a group for NY based writers to discuss the craft and critique our work. We've got a great membership of diverse, insightful writers who bring varied perspectives to the discussion. Each meeting, we spend some time discussing a topic related to the art and craft of writing. Then we workshop 3-4 pieces submitted by members and distributed for reading ahead of time.
