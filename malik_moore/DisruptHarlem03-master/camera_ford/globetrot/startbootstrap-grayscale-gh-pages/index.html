About Globetrot

Have you ever traveled to a new place and felt worried or unprepared? Do you worry about keeping costs low during your trip? Have you found yourself in a sticky situation because you didn’t do your research ahead of time? With globetrot, you can put those problems behind you.

GlobeTrot is a travel companion that helps you find your way, wherever you are. It doesn’t matter if you're on a whirlwind business trip, a solo journey, or vacation with loved ones.

Stay up to date on safety information, cut your travel costs, get curated local advice, and more! From big-picture concerns down to the details of convenience, Globetrot has you covered.

Meet the Team

Camera is a recent graduate of Brown University. After coming home from her first solo trip, she realized that there should be a way to make navigating faraway places as easy as it felt to navigate her native New York City. After much brainstorming, surveying, and reflecting on past travel troubles, Globetrot was born.

Camera loves writing, traveling, and looking at rocks (geology major over here). In her free time, catch her watching How to Get Away With Murder, daydreaming, and learning to code.
